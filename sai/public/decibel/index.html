About

Khøst (pronounced “coast”) is an electronic music DJ/production project curated by Tyler Mead and Grayson Repp – Together they create melodic bass music best described as organic and uplifting. Their freshmen and sophomore releases have gained notoriety amongst industry heavyweights such as: Tiesto, Adventure Club, The Chainsmokers, Vanic, Project 46, as well as a variety of prominent blogs throughout your timeline. 2016 looks to be a year geared towards the Khøst vision with new releases pending…

Tyler Mead

Grayson Repp
